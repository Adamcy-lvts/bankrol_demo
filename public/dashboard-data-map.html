Notes

  • Derived, not stored: the 4 KPIs, funnel totals, referral close-rate, tender win-rate (won ÷ total) and the Needs-Attention list are computed in the service layer.
  • Period filter: the This Month / Quarter / Year toggle date-filters "sold / closed" queries — primarily the Units Sold KPI.
  • Wiring path: DashboardController@index runs the aggregations → returns Inertia props → Welcome.vue reads props instead of the hard-coded v arrays.
